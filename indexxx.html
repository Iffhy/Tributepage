Ada Ameh

1974-2022

About Ada Ameh

  • Ada Grace.O.Ameh was born on May 15 1974 and raised in Ajegunle in Lagos
    State.
  • Ameh received both primary and secondary education in Lagos State but eventually
    quit school at Age 14,the same age she gave birth to her daughter.
  • In 1995 Ameh officially became part of the Nigerian movies industry and received
    her first movie role in 1996 which became quite successful.
  • She featured in movies such as Domitila,the Nigerian TV series titled The Johnsons
    which also became a successful project that recieved awards.
  • She lost several of her family members:most recently her own daughter who she gave birth
    to at age 14.her daughter died in oct 2020.Ameh died after collapsing in Warri on July 17 2022
    at age 48.She suffered from mental health issues prior to her death.

Despite all she went through she tried her best to put smiles on peoples faces.
she ll never be forgotten and sure she's resting in a better place . RIP Ada Ameh
